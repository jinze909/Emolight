Product Introduction

The product is a device that can recognize and alleviate different emotions of a person based on their face expression and color light. The device consists of a camera that captures the face of the person, an artificial intelligence model that analyzes the expression and detects the emotion, and a light-emitting diode (LED) that emits different colors of light according to the emotion. The device can be used in driving, to help the person cope with their emotions and improve their well-being.

Product Advantages

  • The product has several advantages over existing solutions, such as:

    • It can detect emotions that are often hidden or suppressed by the person, which can provide more accurate and reliable feedback.

    It can provide immediate and personalized intervention to alleviate the negative emotions and enhance the positive ones, which can improve the mood and performance of the person.

    It can use natural and non-invasive methods, such as color light, to influence the emotions.
